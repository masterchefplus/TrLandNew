FAQ

How to start a game?
TronLink add-in must be installed at your PC
TronWallet [iOS] or TronWallet [Android] on your Smartphone
Charge up your wallet balance by any cryptocurrency exchange

Gameplay
For a start buy some game coins at a fixed rate 1 TRX = 25 coins.
Then choose the appropriate plantation and pay for it using your game coins.
From this moment your plantation begins to make profit according to its profit-making capacity.

Profit distribution
The whole profit from your plantations equally divides into available funds and treasury funds. Treasury funds can be used to purchase new plantations to expand the production and increase your profit. Available funds can be withdrawn at any moment; they also can be used to buy more plantations.

Profit accrual
The profit from your plantations accrues automatically every hour.

Commission rate
The commission of administration is 10 % of the amount of coins, spent for plantations purchase.

How safe is it?
This project is based on Tron Network smart contract; it is completely decentralized and independent. All operations between participants and contract are absolutely clear.
